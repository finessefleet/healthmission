Thrust Areas

Precision Impact through Structured Engagements

We recognize the importance of exploring the interdisciplinary connections between healthcare, technology, and related fields. Therefore, our initial focus is on the integration of Public Health and Technology.

Digital Health & Health Informatics

Leveraging technology to enhance healthcare delivery, data management, and patient engagement.

AI & Machine Learning in Public Health

Harnessing intelligent systems to improve disease surveillance, diagnostics, and predictive modeling.

Epidemiological Modeling & Data Science

Applying analytical frameworks to understand, forecast, and respond to health challenges.

Global Health & Health Equity

Promoting equitable access to healthcare resources and addressing global disparities in health outcomes.

Environmental & Climate Health

Understanding how environmental factors and climate change affect public health and wellbeing.

Behavioral & Social Health Analytics

Using behavioral insights and social data to design more effective public health interventions.
